Zen is a crypto-currency designed and operated for conducting completely private transactions and communications worldwide.

There are many places in the world where people don’t have freedom and property rights, where slavery still exists, where people can’t conduct business without oversight, and where the national currency does not work anymore.

In some of these countries, like Venezuela, people turn to Bitcoin to be able to buy food, medicine, and other basic necessities. Unfortunately people who use Bitcoin can be tracked by the communications that happen while using Bitcoin, like texting, email, or web browsing.

In other countries, regular hard-working people's wealth is stolen every day through currency inflation, oppressive taxes, and property confiscation.

The Zen design team looked at all aspects of how information leaks when conducting Bitcoin transactions, and addressed them during the design process. Secure communications between nodes and wallets, secure nodes, secure wallets, and secure transactions are all part of the system.

Zen extends traditional crypto-currencies with secure person-to-person communications built into the wallet.

Zen will have outreach and localization teams to make the Zen wallets available for people around the world. Zen will provide oppressed people the ability to communicate and transact securely without the worry of interference. With a limited coin supply and low transaction fee, Zen is an attractive alternative to fiat currencies.

Zen has a strong heritage of software development, inheriting the best of Zclassic, Zcash, and Bitcoin, and extended by inclusive governance, secure operations, and an active community.
